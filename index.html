GULAB JAMUN

Ingredients


  • Milk powder
  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Fresh Cream
  • Ghee
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Oil
  • Dry Fruits

Recipe


  1. MAKE THE DOUGH: Make the proper dough for jamun.
  2. FORM BALL: Make circular ball size jamuns.
  3. FRY THE GULAB JAMUN: Fry the jamuns at proper heat.
  4. MAKE THE SUGAR SYRUP: Dissolve the ssugar in water and heat it.
  5. SOAK THE GULAB JAMUN: Dip the jamuns in sugar syrup.
  6. SERVE AND ENJOY!

SHAHI TUKDA

Ingredients


  • Milk
  • Bread
  • Ghee
  • Sugar
  • Elaichi Powder
  • Dry Fruits

Recipe


  1. MAKE RABDI:Bring milk to boil while stirring. Add bread crumbs. Make it cool.
  2. FRY BREADS: Fry the breads at low heat.
  3. MAKE SUGAR SYRUP: Dissolve the sugar in hot water.
  4. SERVE AND ENJOY!
